×Benefits×

Resurgence Campus Ambassador Program is your way to connect to North India's largest cultural festival 'Resurgence' hosted by the Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University. Register yourself to enjoy special privileges at Resurgence.

Advantages of being a Campus Ambassador:

1. A certificate of appreciation to boost your CV.
2. You get a chance to promote Resurgence 2K14 at your institute and learn some management skills.
3. An opportunity to interact with campus ambassadors all over India and build a strong network.
4. Special privileges during Resurgence 2K14.
5. Free accomodation and registration for fest if you bring a team of at least 10 from your college.
6. Performance based incentives (like letter of recommendation, resurgence merchandise, etc.) So don't wait.
